The Three-Year Academic Program

A systematic journey through Hebrew grammar, syntax, and exegesis.

Year 1

Foundation & Phonology

Focus on the Hebrew alphabet, vowels (Niqqud), pronunciation, and basic vocabulary. Students learn nouns, adjectives, pronouns, and the fundamentals of strong verbs to begin translating simple sentences.

Year 2

Grammar & Syntax

Deepen your understanding with weak verb classifications, complex syntax, and historical grammar. Start reading and analyzing direct narrative passages from the Torah and historical books.

Year 3

Exegesis & Translation

Master biblical poetry, prophetic literature, and advanced textual criticism. Focus on independent translation, literary analysis, and interpreting nuanced Hebrew syntax and theological themes.
